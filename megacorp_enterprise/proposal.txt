! Proposal: Branch LAN AS-path prepend — a policy cascade across the WAN
! Device: branch-rtr-01
! Prepend the AS-path on the branch's advertised LAN (a routine traffic-steering
! edit). Two ASes away, across the WAN transit, a campus-core inbound filter keys
! on the over-prepended path and drops 10.50.1.0/24 outright — so the campus loses
! reachability to the branch LAN. A policy change at the source cascades into a
! router whose own config never changed; you cannot read cam-core in isolation.
route-map TO-PEER permit 10
 match ip address prefix-list RFC1918
 set as-path prepend 65501 65501
