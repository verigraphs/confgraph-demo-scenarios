! Proposal: Branch LAN AS-path prepend — a policy cascade across the WAN
! Device: branch-rtr-01
! Prepend the AS-path on the branch's advertised LANs (a routine traffic-steering
! edit). It lengthens the path for all four branch segments network-wide; two ASes
! away, across the WAN transit, the campus cores import only some of them and their
! inbound filter keys on the over-prepended path — so the campus loses reachability
! to the branch routes it carries while the rest of the fabric just re-routes. A
! policy change at the source cascades into routers whose own config never changed;
! you cannot read cam-core in isolation.
route-map TO-PEER permit 10
 match ip address prefix-list RFC1918
 set as-path prepend 65501 65501
